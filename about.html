About Flame Grill

We bring together time-honored grilling tradition, luxurious atmosphere, and contemporary hospitality.

Our Story

From humble smokehouse roots to a refined grill destination

Flame Grill Restaurant began as a family-run barbecue house and has evolved into a signature dining experience where fire, flavor, and hospitality meet.

2009 — First opened with a 20-seat smokehouse kitchen.
2016 — Expanded into a full-service steakhouse and event venue.
2026 — Recognized for excellence in premium grill dining and catering.

Mission

To create unforgettable meals by combining premium ingredients with modern fire-cooking techniques.

Vision

To become the region’s most celebrated destination for steakhouse-level barbecue and hospitality.

Awards

Best Grill Dining 2024, Gold Service Award 2023, and featured in culinary travel guides.
